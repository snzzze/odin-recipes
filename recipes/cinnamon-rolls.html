Jellycat Cinnamon Rolls

Description

This is how to make these very yummy and very cute Jellycat cinnamon rolls. You would be lying if you said that you've never looked at a Jellycat and thought, "Wow! That looks so good, I wish I could eat it!". Well guess what? Now you can with this super awesome recipe!

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/4 cup fine white sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 7 oz soft unsalted butter
  • 7 oz milk
  • 4.5 cups bread flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp instant yeast

Filling

  • 1 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 5 oz soft unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon

Topping

  • 1 small tub of black decorative icing
  • 1 can of cream cheese frosting

Making the Dough

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl and mix for a few minutes.
  2. Knead the dough until smooth.
  3. Let rise in a bowl for 1 hour!

Making the Filling

  1. Combine the softened butter with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  2. Put in the fridge to cool. This will make it easier to cut without spilling out much.

Putting Everything Together

  1. Roll out the proofed dough to about 90 x 45cm.
  2. Spread the filling on the top, not putting too much.
  3. Roll up like a snail and use a string to cut out your cinnamon rolls.
  4. Save parts of the ends of the dough to make tiny ball feet!
  5. Roll up 8 small dough balls, covering thoroughly in cocoa powder.
  6. Add the rolls and two feet to each roll.

Baking the Rolls!

Place into a square pan spaced apart plenty (I always get about 4-6 large rolls). Any larger pan size works for these, just make sure they are spaced apart. No need to proof again. Bake at 375F for about 30-35min. Decorate a face with black icing! Add cream cheese frosting to top if you like while hot! Enjoy!
